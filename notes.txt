4 types d'ingredients :
- regular food
- status food (w/ a bonus effect)
- critters & insects
- monster parts

3 types de plats :
- basic: fill hearts
- elixirs: give buff
- status dishes: fill hearts & give buff

regular + regular = basic
critters + monsters = elixir
regular + status = status dish

food + food = OK
critters + monster = OK
Le reste = KO
  ex: food + critters/monster = KO
      critters + critters = KO

(
  Emergency cooking pot: Drop wood + flint, then hit it.
  Critical success: sound effect. Guaranteed when blood moon or by adding a star fragment
)

basic dish + status food = status dish
nuts + elixir = elixir + hearts
Mais 2 effets ou plus = annule l'effet.

Elixirs:
- quality of the buff	(low, mid, high) = depends on the critter
- time it lasts		(..) = depends on the # of monster parts

Effects don't stack
