Cooking Simulator

Zelda BOTW: Cooking Simulator is a personal side-project. It brings together every information I have gatehered so far about the cooking mechanism in the game. Its main feature is to allow you to cook dishes just like in the game to try some recipes out before putting them into practice!

Who am I ?

I am 18, study programming and love it. I've always been into Zelda games, and cooking in Zelda Breath of the Wild gave me the incentive to create this website.

Contributing

Any help is welcome!

  • Ingredients statistics and properties: amount of hearts it adds, duration it adds, level of the asociated effect. Look at the sources to figure exactly what is expected for the ingredient to be usable. Even partial information will help as some is currently missing.
  • Images, icons: you can send images of ingredients/meals (80x80), or effects (20x20).
Note: Every image must be a transparent PNG file.
